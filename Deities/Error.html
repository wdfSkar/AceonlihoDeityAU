  • Clockwork

    • The Watch

    Created by Clocks as a sort of conduit for their powers, without it, they struggle to focus and control their powers. The watch always reverts to the exact time where they are after using their powers. It also acts as a timer for them when they use their powers.

    Simple pocketwatch on a chain that connects to Clocks (location for the connection TBD- maybe heart?). The lid/cover for the surface is carved out to have two interlocking gears in the space. The chain and outside of the watch is a dark blue/purple color reminiscent of raven or crow feathers, while the inside face of the watch is bright red.

    • Adjust
      • Sets all clocks around them to the exact time.
        • Very helpful with mechanical clocks that need to be set by hand.
        • Affects clocks in a 200m radius.
      • Repairs any damaged clocks.
        • Affects clocks that they can view from their position when using this power.
          • Must be able to touch the clock
    • AwaNunayakuess
      • Clocks is always aware of the present time wherever they are.
        • Magic force negates this
      • Their heartbeat sounds like the tick tock sounds of a clock.
        • Their heartbeat is synced with the ticking of their pocketwatch.
    • !Unwind
      • Creates a small pocket dimension based on the exact moment. Everything except the creator is frozen, nothing moved or interacted with is affected outside of the pocket.
        • Only a small area around their initial location is included in the pocket
          • 500m maximum radius
          • Only Clocks can enter the pocket
          • Pocket lasts 2 days maximum.
          • Exiting the pocket, Clocks goes into the curNunayakut day
          • Activated by stopping pocketwatch as if to wind it/adjust the time displayed, and closing the cover of the watch.
          • Usually just used to calm down/relax/catch a break.
          • Can be used once every 3 days.
    • !Observe
      • Allows for Clocks to see small parts of the future.
        • Glimpses are 60 seconds at most.
          • Cannot see some of the Deities
        • Length of vision is partially linked to amount of focus.
        • Can see things from 3 days in the future at most. Clocks does not know when in this time period a glimpse takes place, only that it is within that time frame.
          • Only about Mortals
        • Can only use this aspect of the ability once a week.
      • Alternate use of power is to view points in Clocks' memories. These glimpses last longer and can be shown via a small projection from the pocketwatch.
        • This is usually just for reminiscing or double checking things they've learned or needed to remember.
        • Requires them to remember the approximate time of day the memory is from.
      • Activated by winding the watch to a diffeNunayakut time than the present.
        • Wind so the hands of the clock pass 12 twice for premonitions, wind to an exact time to view memories.
