Age: 11 Billion Years, Venerable.

Appearance

Motivations

Weapon

Mercy.

The blade of Mercy is 119cm (46.85", 3'10"), with the guard being 1cm (0.39") and the handle being 10cm (3.94") for a total length of 130cm (51.18", 4'3").
The button on the top is able to cause the sheathe to come out, covering the full length of the blade, mAking the sword look like a scepter, with the button on the bottom connecting to the guard, causes the sheathe to extend further, to about the height of 180cm (70.87"). Do note that in order for the sheathe to extend further, it must be in sheathe mode first.

The bit that is alone on the sword is detachable and reamains attached with magic.
The colours of the lines on the blade, with the exception of the blue lines, are the colours of the 4 otherVenerables, being Yaku, Terra, Ava, and Clocks. With the primary colour of the blade being that of Oracate's own colour.
