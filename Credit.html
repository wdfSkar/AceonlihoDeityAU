The person who creates and modifies this site is me, Skar, and I do my best to have all the information present, but certain artworks/stories can only be found in Aceonliho. This is due to the individual or individuals behind those pieces not wanting it put on here, and as such will not add it without explicit permission from the individual/s in question.

The primary units used on this site are metric, but for your convience, I have included both metric and imperial units; on the Universal Powers page, I have included nonstandard units that involve celestial bodies (Earth, White Dwarf Stars) because the numbers are so grand that I saw it fit to include a real-world example to act as a scale.

The exception to this, is under the Duribility section of the Universal Powers page, where the primary units were scientific units, however both metric (if different from scientific units) and imperial units have still been included; and under the Physical Strength of the Universal Powers page, I had used a celestial body in place of imperial units due to the conversion resulting in too large of a number to indicate any difference from metric.


Any and all art made for deity appearances, weapons, companions, etc. were made by the people behind the deities themselves in the Aceonliho discord server, and in the Deity Overview page, they are done by the person they are under; Any art for the deities, if not done by the person behind them, will be specified otherwise.
