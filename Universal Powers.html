Powers

  • Immortality

  • Regeneration

    • Enhanced Regeneration
      • Capable of regenerating any injury, including lost limbs and scars.
        • Note: Some deities will not regenerate scars or lost limbs, as seen with Oracate.
        • Note: Limbs and scars require more effort to regenerate completely.
    • Pseudo-Invulnerability
      • When hit by weapons mortal in nature, the damage done is so miniscule that it appears as though the deity is invunerable.

  • Physical Strength

    • Venerables
      • These deities are capable of near-unmatched physical strength, and are generally able to throw mortals 1000km (621.37 miles), and able to throw other deities up to 200 kilometers (124.27 miles).
      • These deities can lift up to 2.985×1022 tonnes (5 Earths).
    • The Created
      • These deities are much weaker than the Venerables only able to throw mortals up to 50km (31.07 miles) and can only throw other deities up to 10 meters (32.81').
      • These deities can lift up to 5.97×1023 tonnes (0.1 Earth).

  • Speed

    • Deities have extremely fast reflexes able to dodge bullets.

  • Duribility

    • Deities are able to withstand 44229876.5403atm (4481592240445.90 pascals or 645000000psi) of pressure.
    • Deities are able to withstand 7x1010 Newtons (311387900356lbs) of force.
    • Deities are able to withstand a temperature range of 2.7-2197845897.15K (-270.45 — 2197845624°C, -454.81 — 3956122155.2°F, 2.7K — 21978.46 White Dwarfs).
